About Track Insights

Welcome to Track Insights, your go-to app for IHSAA track and field playoff performances and data insights. Our goal is to promote Indiana high school track and field, motivate athletes, celebrate achievements, and provide insights for fans, coaches, and athletes.

The inspiration for this app came from a single question posed by an athlete:

“I wonder how our 4x800 time would place in other Sectionals?”

While the athlete began manually checking other Sectionals, the process was tedious and only partially completed. We realized this was a perfect task for a computer, which led to the creation of our first program. From there, many more ideas emerged, and we began developing additional programs. This app serves as a platform to share our work with you.

We hope you enjoy using Track Insights!

Key Features

01

Athlete Dashboards: View athlete IHSAA performance marks in one easy-to-read place.

02

Achievement Badges: View athlete badges for achievements.

03

Performance Rankings: See how athlete performances rank across categories such as grade level and school size.

04

Advanced Queries: Explore data insights and track trends in IHSAA playoff performances.
